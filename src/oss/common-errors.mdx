This page contains guides around resolving common errors you may find while building with LangChain and LangGraph.

Errors referenced below will have an `lc_error_code` property corresponding to one of the below codes when they are thrown in code.

* [`GRAPH_RECURSION_LIMIT`](/oss/langgraph/errors/GRAPH_RECURSION_LIMIT)
* [`INVALID_CHAT_HISTORY`](/oss/langgraph/errors/INVALID_CHAT_HISTORY)
* [`INVALID_CONCURRENT_GRAPH_UPDATE`](/oss/langgraph/errors/INVALID_CONCURRENT_GRAPH_UPDATE)
* [`INVALID_GRAPH_NODE_RETURN_VALUE`](/oss/langgraph/errors/INVALID_GRAPH_NODE_RETURN_VALUE)
* [`INVALID_PROMPT_INPUT`](/oss/langchain/errors/INVALID_PROMPT_INPUT)
* [`INVALID_TOOL_RESULTS`](/oss/langchain/errors/INVALID_TOOL_RESULTS)
* [`MESSAGE_COERCION_FAILURE`](/oss/langchain/errors/MESSAGE_COERCION_FAILURE)
* [`MISSING_CHECKPOINTER`](/oss/langgraph/errors/MISSING_CHECKPOINTER)
* [`MODEL_AUTHENTICATION`](/oss/langchain/errors/MODEL_AUTHENTICATION)
* [`MODEL_NOT_FOUND`](/oss/langchain/errors/MODEL_NOT_FOUND)
* [`MODEL_RATE_LIMIT`](/oss/langchain/errors/MODEL_RATE_LIMIT)
* [`MULTIPLE_SUBGRAPHS`](/oss/langgraph/errors/MULTIPLE_SUBGRAPHS)
* [`OUTPUT_PARSING_FAILURE`](/oss/langchain/errors/OUTPUT_PARSING_FAILURE)
